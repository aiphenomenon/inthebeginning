## Key Decisions

- **Tempo range 1.1x-2.1x**: Slower than v8's 1.5x-2.5x, giving more breathing
  room for the expanded instrument set. Density awareness prevents cacophony
  during busy simulation epochs.
- **Family pools as separate dict entries**: Rather than stuffing everything into
  the existing 5 families, we add dedicated pools for rock, synth, world, sax,
  choir. The instrument selector can draw from any pool.
- **Variety enforcement**: Track which family pools have been used across segments;
  if a pool hasn't appeared by the halfway point, bias selection toward it.
- **Backward compatibility**: v7 and v8 remain unchanged. v9 is additive only.
