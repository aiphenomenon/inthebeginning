### Root Cause
V12's `_render_segment` applies `GainStage.master_limit(knee=0.80)` per-segment,
then the streaming writer applies `_soft_limit(0.8)` again. This double compression
creates bitcrusher-like artifacts. V8 has no per-segment limiting.
