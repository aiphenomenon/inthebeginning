### Actions Taken

#### Radio Engine v7 (`apps/audio/radio_engine.py`)

1. **Tempo multiplier**: Changed `_compute_tempo_multiplier()` from 2.0-4.0x to 1.5-2.5x
2. **Instruments per mood**: Changed `MoodSegment.n_instruments` from 6-16 to 2-4
3. **Mood duration**: Changed from `uniform(42, 188)` to weighted choice from
   `[42, 84, 84, 126, 126, 168, 210]` (multiples of 42s)
4. **Rondo patterns**: Expanded from 2 (ABACA, ABACADA) to 7 patterns:
   ABACA, ABACADA, ABCBA, AABBA, ABCDA, ABACBA, AABA
5. **Arpeggio forms**: Added 6 forms (block, ascending, descending, alberti, broken,
   pendulum) applied per-section for variety
6. **Anti-click processing**:
   - 2ms micro-fade on each synthesized note (attack + release guard)
   - DC offset removal on each loop buffer
   - 8kHz gentle lowpass on all loop output
   - 2-4 second cosine crossfade at loop boundaries (was 0.5-1s linear)
   - Capped effective tempo at 360 BPM (was 480)
7. **Hartmann consonance**: Enhanced chord building to reject tritones against bass,
   enforce consonant intervals between voices
8. **Instrument family diversity**: Replaced 50/50 piano/orchestra coin flip with
   weighted family pools (strings, brass, woodwinds, keys, pitched_perc) — each
   voice draws from a different family
9. **Voice rendering**: Each voice gets a persistent coloring instrument per section,
   builds its own chord voicing at its register, all notes snapped to scale
10. **Segment planning**: Updated `_plan_segments()` to use 42s multiples,
    max segment buffer raised to 220s
